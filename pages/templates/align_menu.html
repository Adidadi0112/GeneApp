Align Sequences

Here are several methods for sequence aligning analysis

Dot plot

Visual alignment tool revealing sequence similarities and differences through the presence of dots at matching positions.

Try it!
Needlman-Wunsch

The Needleman-Wunsch algorithm is a dynamic programming method used for global alignment of two sequences, which finds the optimal alignment by minimizing the alignment cost or maximizing the similarity between the sequences.

Try it!
MSA

MSA(Multiple Sequence Alignment) algorithm aligns multiple sequences, revealing conserved regions and structural similarities. It aids in understanding evolutionary relationships and has applications in diverse fields such as evolutionary biology and protein structure prediction.
