Unlock the Potential of DNA and Protein Analysis with GeneApp!


The purpose of this website is to gather publicly available tools for the analysis of DNA and protein sequences and enable users to conveniently utilize them. The available functions include: searching for protein IDs by entering a sequence, retrieving DNA sequences by entering protein IDs, comparing sequences, and generating a three-dimensional structure of the entered protein.
